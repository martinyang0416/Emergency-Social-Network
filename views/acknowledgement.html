Welcome to the community!

The Emergency Social Network (ESN) is designed to serve as a dedicated platform for civilians to use during emergency situations.

Unlike typical social networks, ESN is tailored specifically to support small communities severely impacted by natural disasters such as earthquakes, tsunamis, tornadoes, wildfires, and more.

Its primary focus is on providing effective communication and assistance in times of crisis.
